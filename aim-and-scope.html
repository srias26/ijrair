Aim And Scope

Aim and Scope of the Journal

International Journal of Recent Innovations in Academic Research (IJRIAR) is a multidisciplinary International Journal which aims are to promote the current advancements in the area of Basic and Applied Sciences, Health Sciences, Humanities, Social Sciences, Agriculture Sciences and also Engineering and Technology.

Types of articles for Publication

Research articles, Review articles, Survey Papers, Short Communications, Case studies, Technical Reports, Comparative Studies, Dissertations, M.Tech.,/M.E.,/M.Sc.,/M.Phil.,/Ph.D. Thesis.

Basic and Applied Sciences (Life and Biological Sciences)

Basic and Applied Sciences (Life and Biological Sciences), Biotechnology, Biochemistry, Environmental Sciences, Immunology, Molecular Biology, Cell Biology, Genetics, Microbiology, Cytology, Pathology, Nanotechnology, Earth Sciences, Geology and Geography, Industrial Microbiology, Animal Biotechnology, Wild Life Management, Nutrition, Food Sciences, Limnology, Ichthyology, Biodiversity and Conservation, Physics and Astronomy, Material Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Applied Mathematics, Biophysics and other related Sciences.

Health Sciences

Neuroscience and Behavioural Health, Medicine and Dentistry, Case Studies Related to Medicine and Dentistry, Medical Practices, Oncology, Clinical Studies, Diagnostic Reviews, Epidemiology, Public Health, Toxicology, Pharmacological Sciences, Health Professions, Nursing, Veterinary Sciences, Veterinary Medicine, Endocrinology, Diabetics, Auto-Immune Diseases, Vitamin Deficiency Diseases, Diabetes, Nutritional Disorders, Urology, Nephrology, Gastroenterology, Asthma Research, Intestinal Cancer Studies and other related subjects of health sciences.

Social Science and Humanities

Finance, Accounting, Hotel Management, Tourism, Education, Economic Laws, Anthropology, Marketing, Population Studies, Cross culture Studies, Strategic Management, Business Ethics, Business Studies, Corporate Governance, Media Studies, Library Science, International Relations, Psychology, Methodology, Political Science, Philosophy, Women Studies, Visual Arts, Religious Studies, Linguistics and Poetics, Social Welfare, Agriculture Business, Rural Development, Corporate Agriculture, Agro-Marketing, Market Value Chains, Organization Management Studies, Communication Studies, Research and Development Establishments, Human Resource Management, Operations Management, English, Hindi, Sanskrit, History, Political Science, HR Management, Public Administration, Journalism, Media Analysis, Law and Practices, Policy Research.

Agriculture Sciences

Agriculture Economics, Animal Sciences, Forestry Sciences, Agriculture Extension, Agronomy-Plant Science, Plant Breeding, Horticulture, Water Use, Plant Fertilization, Soil Science, Aquaculture, Fishery Sciences, Agricultural Engineering, Biological Engineering, Impacts of pesticides on Agriculture, Food Science, Animal Husbandry, Irrigation, Water Management, Water Use, Waste Management, Plant Diseases, Natural Resources, Industrial Impact on Agriculture, Rural and Agriculture Sociology, Information Technology in Agriculture and other related studies.

Engineering and Technology

Communications and Network, Computer Science and its Applications to Engineering Sciences, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Building Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics, Telecommunications, Technology Management, Construction Engineering, Engineering Mathematics, Optical Engineering, Molecular Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Acoustical Engineering, Bioprocess Engineering, Information Technology, Architecture Engineering, Industrial Engineering, Manufacture Engineering, Software and Biological Engineering, Remote Sensing Studies, Graphics and Multimedia and Infrastructure Engineering and other related studies.
