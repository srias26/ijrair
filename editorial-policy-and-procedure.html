Editorial Policy And Procedure

Editor-in-Chief of IJRIAR

Check the submitted manuscript for format, Originality, Suitability of the paper and finally scope of the Journal. If authors failed to submit without following the guidelines of the Journal may be rejected or returned to the Corresponding author to set the paper in Journal format then only accepted for consideration.

After receiving a manuscript the Technical Editorial staff will conduct the Plagiarism with iThenticate or Turnitin software before submitting the papers to Reviewers or Editors. If Plagiarism is found in the submitted articles, we won't consider for publication.

After Review process the recommendations of the Editors or reviewers panel write to the Editor-In -Chief Editor should be one of the following

  • Accept the publication as such-No amendments are required in the manuscript
  • Accept the publication-after minor amendments mentioned by Reviewers
  • Accept the Publication-after major amendments suggested by Reviewers
  • Reject the publication
