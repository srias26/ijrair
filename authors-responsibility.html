Authors Responsibilities of IJRIAR

Reporting Standards: Authors should follow the manuscript submission guidelines of the journal and disclose the information by stating that the submitting manuscript was not submitted for publication previously in any other Journals. The content of the manuscript should quoted properly, and should not use the ideas of the others, if you want to use the ideas you must acknowledge the source through appropriate references or citations.

Plagiarism: The journal aimed to publish plagiarism free articles, authors are strongly recommending to check the plagiarism policies of the journal mentioned in our web, before submitting a manuscript to our journal.

Authorship of the Paper: Authorship in the manuscript should limited to those who made a prominent contribution to design, interpretation of the data and others must be listed as co-authors. Corresponding author take consent of the co-authors before submitting paper to our Journal.

Fundamental Errors in the Submitted Paper: Authors will noticed any basic errors in the submitted paper in due course of time, any one of the author of the paper should report the Editor-in-Chief by mentioning Manuscript number or paper title, so that we will take measurable actions.

Withdrawal policy of the Submitted Paper: If any author wish to withdrawn your submitted manuscript inform us before review has been started. We should not allow you to withdrawn the manuscript after review process initiated, in such cases authors have to pay the necessary processing charges towards your submitted article.
