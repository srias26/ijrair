Instructions to Authors

Copyright Form     Model Article file

Submission of manuscripts

Manuscript written in English should be in Ms word format and font of manuscript typed in Times New Roman, 12 with 1.5 line spacing throughout the manuscript. Before submission the corresponding author should be declare that it has not been submitted previously in any other peer-reviewed journals and should accepted by all authors on behalf of corresponding author. If any complaints received against the published content Editor-in-Chief will ask clarification to the corresponding author.

Electronic Format:

Please submit your valuable manuscript as an electronic version (in MS Word) to (editor.riar@gmail.com). The manuscript should be sent as single file along with Tables and Figures.

Note: For general queries related to manuscript submission, copyright forms, payment procedures, status of article, model manuscript, plagiarism policy, ethical issues, and other please contact our managing editor at: (editor.recentinnovations@gmail.com)

The manuscript submission for Research Articles the following sequence should be followed Title Page including E-mail Address and valid contact number, Abstract, Keywords, Introduction, Material and Methods, Results and Discussion (Either it combined or separated according to convenience of the authors), Acknowledgements, Conclusions, References.

Title Page:   Authors Current Affiliation with Name, Designation, Department, University/College/Research Station Name, Place, Country, Pin Code Active E-mail Id, Contact Number.

Abstract:
Keywords: A maximum of six keywords are allowed, each keyword is separated by comma (Example: Protein, Lipid, Carbohydrate, Moisture etc.........).

Introduction:
It should be stated that, the aim of the current investigation and statements with previous work which are relevant to the theme of the manuscript

Materials and Methods
Clear and detailed methodology should be given. If your manuscript contains new methods which should be useful for reproducibility of the work. Statistical tools if any mention in the methodology section. Known methods are available which should be quoted with proper citations.

Results:
Results should be clear and concise. Presentation of results with tables, figures and statistical interpretation of the data may improve its clarity.

Discussion
Explains the significance of the results and compare your results with previous studies reported elsewhere which gives additional information towards scientific field.

Important Note
Results and Discussion can be separated or combined according to your convenience.

Conclusion
Conclusion should be made with summary of results interpretation and possible suggested recommendations for future research and should not be the repeat point made in the preceding sections of the manuscript.

Acknowledgements:
In this section you have to mention acknowledgements of personal, institutional and funding agencies and should be kept to the absolute minimum

Tables and Figures
Tables and Figures should be numbered consecutively with Arabic numerals as Table 1, Table 2 and Table 3, Figure 1, Figure 2 and Figure 3 and so on..... table tile should be placed above the table. Short title of the table should be preferable and the source of the table should be mentioned at the bottom. Suitable caption for the Figures should be on the bottom

References
While preparing manuscript the citations mentioned in text are given by author name and yea-for single author (Sairam, 1987). In case of two authors both names and year of publication (Sindu and Bindu, 1999). In case of more than two authors or multiple authors cited by the Name of the First author followed by 'et al.,' (Prasanthi Reddy et al., 2009). If you want to mention group of references, they should be quoted chronological order (For Example: Many reports are available on the variations in Physico-chemical properties of Estuaries (Karthik Kuamr, 2012; Siva, 2013; Rubiya et al., 2014; Meghana, 2015; Mamatha et al., 2016).

Abbreviations and Units in the Text:

Authors are requested to define the abbreviations in your manuscript which are using first time. Standard SI units should be used. See the example as mentioned below. E.g. Enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay (ELISA) and Running Mortality Syndrome (RMS).

Copyright: After review process completed, the papers which are recommended for publication by the editorial staff authors will be asked to submit copyright transfer. A model copyright form is available in Journal Website. Authors are requested to download the sample copy right Agreement form and same should be submitted to Editor-in-Chief via email: (editor.riar@gmail.com)

Proofs and Reprints

Soft copy of the final paper will be sent to the corresponding author via email. Authors are allowed to make the necessary changes in the manuscript at proof stage like change of author positions, grammatical errors and other small amendments. Major corrections at this stage should not be considered. Authors should submit the final gallery proof at (editor.riar@gmail.com) within 4 days after receipt. If someone failed to submit the gallery proof in specified time inform to the Editor-in-Chief and such articles will be published in next issue.

Manuscript Submission Checklist:

Before submitting manuscript to IJRIAR, authors are requested to check the following contents The submission has not been previously published, nor is it before another journal for consideration The submitted Manuscript is in Microsoft word format Text in the manuscript should be 1.5 spaced Use Times New Roman Font (12) throughout the manuscript Species names should be in italics Figures and tables are consecutively numbered and placed within the text at the appropriate positions Article with free of plagiarism.
