Welcome to IJRAIR

International Journal of Recent Innovations in Academic Research (IJRIAR) is an open access, peer-reviewed, multidisciplinary International Journal, that provides rapid publication (Monthly) of research articles, review articles and short communications in all subjects: Basic and Applied Sciences, Health Sciences, Humanities, Social Sciences, Agriculture Sciences and also Engineering and Technology.

Benefits of Publishing in IJRIAR
  • IJRIAR is an International, Monthly, Peer reviewed, Multidisciplinary, Open Access Journal
  • Research findings will publish immediately in current issue after thorough review process
  • IJRIAR accepts original and high quality, research and technical papers in all basic and applied sciences, Humanities, Arts, Management and Technology.
  • Authors can download their full length articles at any time
